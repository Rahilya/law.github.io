WelegalAdvisors.com Disclaimer

WelegalAdvisors.com is a website dedicated to serving the public as an informational resource, providing accurate content about various medical treatments and associated side effects. WelegalAdvisors.com also connects users with its legal partners, who can evaluate whether an individual has a legal case as a result of side effects or complications caused by a medical treatment.

WelegalAdvisors.com is not affiliated with any pharmaceutical companies or drug manufacturers and does not accept advertising or host online advertisements.

There may be times when a visitor comes to WelegalAdvisors.com looking for assistance with a drug or medical device and We legal Advisors determines that visitor would be better served by a trusted outside service provider or law firm. In those cases, the visitor may be contacted directly by that provider or firm.

CONSENT TO CONTACT

By submitting a form about a specific drug or medical device, you consent to be contacted by a We legal Advisors representative or a representative from the appropriate service provider. Any information provided to WelegalAdvisors.com will be shared with the service provider. UPDATED WITH LATEST INFORMATION

WelegalAdvisors.com is continually updated to ensure current information about medications and medical devices and associated side effects is provided to the public. Content found on WelegalAdvisors.com should not be taken as medical advice and site visitors are encouraged to speak with a medical professional for medical treatment, information and recommendations.
